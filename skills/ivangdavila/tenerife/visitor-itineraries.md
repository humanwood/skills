# Visitor Itineraries — Tenerife

## 1-Day Highlights

Best if you only have one day (cruise ship, layover).

### Morning (8:00-13:00)
1. **Start early at La Laguna** — Coffee at Café Teatro, walk historic center
2. **Drive to Anaga** — Stop at Mirador Cruz del Carmen (45 min from La Laguna)
3. **Quick Anaga taste** — Short walk on Sendero de los Sentidos (20 min loop)

### Midday (13:00-15:30)
4. **Lunch at Taganana** — Fresh fish at Casa África or El Frontón
5. **Playa de las Teresitas** — Quick dip or walk on golden beach (30 min stop)

### Afternoon (15:30-21:00)
6. **Drive to Teide** — Via La Esperanza road (1.5h to cable car base)
7. **Teide views** — Even without cable car, the National Park landscapes are incredible
8. **Sunset at Roques de García** — Iconic volcanic formations, best light at sunset

**Or alternatively (beach/south focus):**
- Morning: Teide cable car (book 10am)
- Lunch: Vilaflor (highest village)
- Afternoon: Los Gigantes boat trip or Costa Adeje beach
- Evening: Dinner at La Caleta fishing village

## 7-Day Complete Experience

### Day 1: Arrival & South Coast
Arrive, pick up car (essential), ease into island rhythm.
- Settle into accommodation (Costa Adeje/El Médano recommended)
- Afternoon: Beach time at Playa del Duque or El Médano
- Evening: Sunset at Montaña Roja (easy 30 min hike)
- Dinner: La Caleta fishing village

### Day 2: Mount Teide (Full Day)
Dedicate a full day to Spain's highest peak.
- Early start (before clouds form)
- Cable car + optional summit hike (if permit obtained)
- Explore Roques de García and lunar landscapes
- Lunch at Parador
- Optional: Stay for sunset and stargazing (book observatory tour)

### Day 3: West Coast
- Morning: Boat trip Los Gigantes (whale watching + cliffs)
- Lunch: Garachico, swim in natural pools
- Afternoon: Masca village (drive only if not hiking)
- Evening: Puerto de la Cruz, stay overnight

### Day 4: North Immersion
Based in Puerto de la Cruz:
- Morning: Botanical Garden (Jardín de Aclimatación)
- Mid-morning: La Orotava historic town
- Lunch: Traditional Canarian restaurant in La Orotava
- Afternoon: Loro Parque (allow 4-5 hours)
- Evening: Walk Playa Jardín, dinner in Puerto

### Day 5: Anaga Adventure
Best hiking day.
- Drive to Cruz del Carmen early
- Full hike: Chinamada caves or Roque Taborno loop (4-5h)
- Lunch: Pack picnic or Taganana after
- Afternoon: Beach Teresitas
- Evening: La Laguna tapas and town walk

### Day 6: Culture & Relaxation
Mix of culture and beach.
- Morning: Santa Cruz: Auditorio (Calatrava), Mercado, García Sanabria park
- Lunch: Santa Cruz centro
- Afternoon: Siam Park (if not done) or Playa de las Teresitas
- Evening: La Laguna dinner, university area bars

### Day 7: Your Choice / Departure
Options based on what you loved:
- **More nature**: Teno Rural Park (remote, dramatic)
- **More beach**: El Médano, kitesurfing lesson
- **More food**: Wine route (Tacoronte-Acentejo), guachinche lunch
- **More adventure**: Diving, paragliding from Ifonche
- **Relaxing**: Spa day, pool time before flight

## Practical Timing

### Best Combinations
- **Teide + Stargazing**: One epic day/night
- **Anaga + Teresitas**: Nature + beach same day
- **Garachico + Icod + Puerto**: Nice west-north route
- **La Laguna + Santa Cruz**: Close together, combine easily

### Avoid
- Teide on cloudy day (check forecasts)
- Masca hike on weekends (crowded)
- Multiple long drives same day (roads are slow)
- South beaches at midday August (packed)

### Time Allowances
| Activity | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| La Laguna old town | 2-3 hours |
| Anaga proper hike | 4-6 hours |
| Teide cable car + walks | 4-5 hours |
| Loro Parque | 5-6 hours |
| Siam Park | 4-6 hours |
| Whale watching trip | 3-4 hours |
| Masca gorge hike | 5-6 hours |

### Booking Requirements
- **Teide cable car**: Book online days ahead (especially summer)
- **Teide summit permit**: Apply 2+ months ahead
- **Masca hike**: Book via official system
- **Whale watching**: Book 1-2 days ahead in high season
- **Stargazing tours**: Book 1 week ahead
