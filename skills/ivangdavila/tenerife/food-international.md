# International & Fine Dining — Tenerife

## Fine Dining Scene

Tenerife has more Michelin stars than you'd expect from an island of 900,000 people.

### Michelin-Starred Restaurants

**M.B by Martín Berasategui** ⭐⭐
- Location: Ritz-Carlton Abama
- Style: Basque contemporary
- Price: €180-250 tasting menus
- Reservations: Essential, weeks ahead
- The experience: Technical perfection, valley views

**Kabuki** ⭐
- Location: Ritz-Carlton Abama
- Style: Japanese-Mediterranean fusion
- Price: €120-180
- Reservations: Essential
- Note: Part of Kabuki group

**NUB** ⭐
- Location: La Caleta
- Style: Creative contemporary
- Price: €90-130 tasting menus
- Chefs: Andrea Bernardi & Fernanda Fuentes
- The experience: Intimate, personal, exceptional produce

**El Rincón de Juan Carlos** ⭐
- Location: Los Gigantes
- Style: Modern Canarian
- Price: €80-120
- Brothers Juan Carlos and Jonathan Padrón
- Local ingredients, global technique

### Near-Michelin (Worth Visiting)

**La Vieja** (La Caleta)

**Haydée** (La Orotava)

## Vegetarian & Vegan

Growing scene, especially for nomad communities.

### Dedicated Vegetarian/Vegan
- **Eka Vegan House** (La Laguna): Fully vegan, creative
- **Bora Bora** (El Médano): Health-focused, vegan options
- **Lilium** (La Laguna): Vegetarian, organic
- **El Aguacate** (Puerto Cruz): Vegetarian café

### Vegan-Friendly
- Most Canarian restaurants can adapt
- Papas arrugadas naturally vegan
- Salads and vegetables abundant
- Ask about fish stock in soups

### Health Food
- **Organic markets**: La Laguna Sunday
- **Health stores**: Herbolarios throughout
- **Juice bars**: Growing in south and La Laguna

## Practical Fine Dining Tips

### Reservations
- Michelin restaurants: 2-4 weeks ahead
- Upscale restaurants: 2-3 days ahead
- Weekends: Always book ahead
- Apps: Fork (TheFork) works well

### Dress Code
- Michelin: Smart casual minimum
- Beach restaurants: Casual fine
- Hotels: Usually smart casual
- No strict requirements usually

### Tipping
- Not expected but appreciated
- 5-10% for exceptional service
- Often included in groups

### Wine
- House wine is usually fine
- Local Canarian wines excellent
- Corkage if bringing own (ask)

### Timing
- Lunch: 13:30-15:30
- Dinner: 20:30-22:30
- Earlier possible in tourist areas
- Later okay in Spanish contexts
