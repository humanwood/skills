## Banking

### Main Banks

| Bank | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| Sabadell | Online friendly, English | Branch fees |
| BBVA | Large network | Bureaucratic |
| CaixaBank | Best ATM coverage | Traditional |
| Santander | International | Less flexible |
| ING | No fees, online | No branches |

### Opening Account

**Requirements**
- NIE (essential for most)
- Passport
- Proof of address
- Initial deposit (€100-500)

**Without NIE**
- Difficult
- Non-resident account possible
- Higher fees
- Limited services

### Digital Banks (Easier Start)
- N26: EU-wide, no NIE needed
- Revolut: Good for multi-currency
- Wise: Excellent for transfers
- Not Spanish banks (some limitations)

### Costs
| Service | Typical Fee |
|---------|-------------|
| Account maintenance | €0-6/month |
| Card | €0-40/year |
| ATM (own bank) | Free |
| ATM (other bank) | €0-2 |
| International transfer | €0-20 |

## Post & Shipping

### Correos (Spanish Post)

**Services**
- Standard mail
- Packages
- Post office boxes
- Financial services

**Typical Times**
| Destination | Days |
|-------------|------|
| Within Canaries | 1-3 |
| Mainland Spain | 2-5 |
| EU | 5-10 |
| International | 7-15 |

### Package Delivery

**Carriers**
- Correos Express
- SEUR
- MRW
- DHL, FedEx, UPS

**Note on Canaries**
- Not all carriers deliver to islands
- Shipping costs often higher
- "Free EU shipping" sometimes excludes Canarias
- Check before ordering

### Receiving Packages
- Address format matters
- Include province (Santa Cruz de Tenerife)
- Provide phone number
- Tracking recommended

## Government & Admin

### Key Offices

**Ayuntamiento (Town Hall)**
- Empadronamiento
- Local permits
- Local taxes
- One per municipality

**Oficina de Extranjeros**
- NIE processing
- Foreigner registration
- Santa Cruz main, Puerto de la Cruz satellite

**Agencia Tributaria (Tax Office)**
- Tax matters
- NIE also possible here
- Santa Cruz, La Laguna

### Appointments (Cita Previa)
- Required for most offices
- Book online: sede.administracion.gob.es
- Or call: 060
- Bring all documents
- Arrive early

### Gestorías
- Administrative agents
- Help with paperwork
- Speak English often
- €50-200 per process
- Worth it for complex matters

## Driving & Vehicles

### License
**EU licenses**: Valid indefinitely
**UK (post-Brexit)**: Valid for visits; exchange if resident
**Other**: International permit + home license; exchange if resident

### License Exchange
1. Book appointment at DGT
2. Medical exam (€30-50)
3. Photos
4. Application fee
5. Some countries need test

### Vehicle Registration (ITV)
- Annual inspection required
- Similar to MOT
- Book at ITV station
- €35-50
- Fail = repairs + retest

### Buying a Car
- Transfer at Tráfico
- Gestorías can help
- Check ITV history
- Verify no outstanding fines

## Emergencies Beyond 112

### Breakdown
- RACE: 900 100 992 (automobile club)
- Insurance breakdown line
- Grúa (tow truck) in phone book

### Locksmith
- Cerrajeros (look for 24h)
- Can be expensive late night
- Use police if locked out and secure

### Plumber/Electrician
- Fontanero (plumber)
- Electricista
- Ask neighbors for recommendations
- Get quote first

### Lost Passport
- Report to police
- Contact embassy/consulate
- Emergency travel document available
