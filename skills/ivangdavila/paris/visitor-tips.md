# Visitor Tips & Day Trips — Paris

## Essential Tips

### Money

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **ATMs** | Use bank ATMs ONLY (BNP, Société Générale, Crédit Agricole) |
| **Avoid Euronet** | Private ATMs charge 10-40% hidden fees |
| **Tipping** | Service included; round up or €2-3 for good service |
| **Cash vs Card** | Card accepted almost everywhere; keep €50 cash backup |
| **Exchange** | Never at airports or tourist areas; use ATMs |

### Communication

| Phrase | Pronunciation | When |
|--------|---------------|------|
| **Bonjour** | bohn-ZHOOR | Always greet first |
| **Merci** | mehr-SEE | Thank you |
| **S'il vous plaît** | see voo PLAY | Please |
| **L'addition** | lah-dee-see-OHN | The bill please |
| **Pardon** | par-DOHN | Excuse me |
| **Je ne parle pas français** | zhuh nuh parl pah frahn-SAY | I don't speak French |

**Critical:** Always say "Bonjour" when entering any shop, restaurant, or speaking to anyone. Skipping it is considered extremely rude.

### Etiquette

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Say bonjour/bonsoir | Jump straight to questions |
| Wait to be seated | Seat yourself at restaurants |
| Ask before photos of people | Photograph without permission |
| Dress neatly | Wear athletic clothes to nice restaurants |
| Keep voice down | Be loud in public |

### Saving Money

| Strategy | Savings |
|----------|---------|
| Picnic lunch | €15 saved/day |
| Carafe d'eau (tap water) | €5-10/meal |
| Market croissants | €1 vs €3 at cafés |
| Museum Pass (2/4/6 days) | If doing 4+ museums |
| Happy hour drinks | 50% off |
| Lunch menu du jour | 30% vs dinner |

## Day Trips from Paris

### Top Day Trips

| Destination | Distance | Transport | Time | Cost |
|-------------|----------|-----------|------|------|
| **Versailles** | 20 km | RER C | 40 min | €8 round trip |
| **Giverny** | 80 km | Train + bus | 1.5 hrs | €25 round trip |
| **Champagne (Reims)** | 145 km | TGV | 45 min | €30-50 round trip |
| **Mont Saint-Michel** | 360 km | TGV + bus | 3.5 hrs | €70+ round trip |
| **Loire Castles** | 200 km | Tour or TGV | 2 hrs | €50+ |
| **Disneyland Paris** | 35 km | RER A | 45 min | €17 round trip |

### Versailles Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| Book ahead | chateau-versailles.fr |
| Go Tuesday-Saturday | Closed Monday, crowded Sunday |
| Start with gardens | Palace opens late |
| Bring lunch | Restaurants overpriced |
| Allow full day | Palace + gardens = 5-6 hours |

### Giverny Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| Season | April-October only |
| Best time | Early morning (8:30 opening) |
| Book ticket | fondation-monet.com |
| Combine | With Versailles for full day? No, each is full day |

### Champagne Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| Book tours | Most houses require reservation |
| Top houses | Moët, Veuve Clicquot, Taittinger |
| Best approach | Morning TGV, 2 houses, late train back |
| Drive? | Only if not drinking; car useful for small producers |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Better Approach |
|---------|-----------------|
| Trying to see everything | Focus on 2-3 things/day |
| Eating near attractions | Walk 5-10 min away |
| Not booking Eiffel Tower | Book 60 days ahead |
| Skipping markets | Better food, cheaper |
| Only tourist areas | Explore 10th, 11th, 20th |
| Taking taxis everywhere | Metro is faster, cheaper |
| August visit | Many things closed |
| No French phrases | Learn basics, locals appreciate it |
