# Attractions Guide — Paris

## Worth Your Time

| Attraction | Why | Cost | Tip |
|------------|-----|------|-----|
| **Louvre Museum** | World's largest museum, Mona Lisa | €22 | Book timed entry, go Friday evening |
| **Musée d'Orsay** | Impressionists (Monet, Van Gogh) | €16 | Less crowded than Louvre |
| **Eiffel Tower** | Iconic, must-do once | €11-29 | Book 60 days ahead, stairs cheaper |
| **Sainte-Chapelle** | Stunning stained glass | €13 | Morning light is best |
| **Notre-Dame** | Reopened 2024, Gothic masterpiece | FREE | Long queues, go early |
| **Musée Rodin** | The Thinker, beautiful garden | €14 | Garden-only ticket €6 |
| **Sacré-Cœur** | Views + Montmartre atmosphere | FREE | Walk up, skip funicular |
| **Arc de Triomphe** | Rooftop views of Champs-Élysées | €16 | Sunset timing ideal |
| **Père Lachaise** | Jim Morrison, Oscar Wilde graves | FREE | Get map at entrance |
| **Galeries Lafayette** | Art Deco dome, free rooftop views | FREE | Rooftop for photos |
| **Luxembourg Gardens** | Perfect for picnic, people-watching | FREE | Bring food from nearby bakery |
| **Marais** | Historic Jewish quarter, boutiques | FREE | Sunday browsing best |
| **Canal Saint-Martin** | Hipster area, cafes, bridges | FREE | Afternoon stroll |

## Tourist Traps to SKIP

| Skip | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Euronet/private ATMs** | 10-40% hidden fees | Bank ATMs (BNP, Société Générale) |
| **Restaurants at Eiffel Tower** | Overpriced, bad quality | Walk 10 min to Rue Cler |
| **Moulin Rouge dinner show** | €200+, food mediocre | Just photo outside, see real cabaret |
| **Ladurée macarons** | €30 for 8, tourist tax | Pierre Hermé (better, similar price) |
| **Hop-on-hop-off bus** | €40+, traffic nightmare | Metro + walking far better |
| **Love Lock Bridges** | Locks removed, just vendors | Any other bridge for photos |
| **Montmartre artists** | Aggressive, overpriced sketches | Politely decline, keep walking |
| **Seine cruise with dinner** | Food terrible, €100+ | €16 Bateaux Mouches cruise instead |
| **Galeries Lafayette shopping** | Tourist markup | Same brands cheaper elsewhere |
| **Champs-Élysées restaurants** | Tourist trap central | Side streets in 8th |
| **Latin Quarter "3-course €15"** | Frozen food, bad wine | Real bistros nearby |

## Free Attractions

| Attraction | Best Time | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| **All national museums** | 1st Sunday monthly | Louvre, Orsay, etc. Get there early |
| **Notre-Dame exterior** | Morning | Interior limited capacity |
| **Sacré-Cœur + Montmartre** | Sunset | Amazing views |
| **Galeries Lafayette rooftop** | Any time | 360° views, free |
| **Parc de Belleville** | Sunset | Best free viewpoint |
| **La Défense** | Daytime | Modern architecture |
| **Canal Saint-Martin** | Afternoon | Bridges, locks, cafes |
| **Le Marais streets** | Sunday | Jewish bakeries open |
| **Jardin des Tuileries** | Morning | Between Louvre and Concorde |
| **Palais Royal gardens** | Afternoon | Hidden gem, Buren columns |
