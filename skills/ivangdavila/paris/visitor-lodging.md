# Where to Stay — Paris

## Best Areas by Traveler Type

| Type | Best Areas | Why | Nightly Rate |
|------|------------|-----|--------------|
| **First-time visitor** | 1st, 4th (Marais), 5th, 6th | Central, walkable to sights | €150-300 |
| **Budget traveler** | 10th, 11th, 18th (south) | Affordable, good metro | €80-150 |
| **Luxury** | 6th, 7th, 8th | Upscale, beautiful | €400+ |
| **Families** | 7th, 15th, 12th | Quiet, space, parks | €150-250 |
| **Nightlife** | 10th, 11th, Marais | Bars, restaurants | €100-200 |
| **Business** | 8th, 2nd, 9th | Near offices | €150-300 |

## Booking Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Book 2-3 months ahead** | For peak season (Apr-Oct) |
| **Avoid Fashion Week** | March, September — prices spike |
| **Check breakfast** | Often €15-25 extra; bakery cheaper |
| **Airport transfer** | Ask if included or cost |
| **Wi-Fi** | Free almost everywhere now |
| **A/C** | Not standard; check if summer travel |

---

## Areas to Avoid Staying

| Area | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Châtelet (underground mall area)** | Seedy at night |
| **Gare du Nord immediate area** | Not pleasant |
| **Stalingrad/Porte de la Chapelle** | Safety concerns |
| **Very outer arrondissements** | Long commute, no charm |
| **CDG airport area** | Unless flying early |
