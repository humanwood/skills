| 12:30 | Walk through Tuileries | Exit toward Concorde |
| 13:00 | Lunch near Palais Royal | Affordable options inside arcades |
| 14:30 | Notre-Dame exterior | Recently reopened |

## 7 Days (In-Depth)

### Day 1: Arrival + Orientation
- Check into accommodation
- Neighborhood walk
- Light dinner, early sleep

### Day 2: Louvre + Tuileries
- Full Louvre experience (3-4 hrs)
- Tuileries picnic
- Palais Royal

### Day 3: Eiffel Tower + West
- Trocadéro photos (morning light)
- Eiffel Tower (book ahead)
- Champ de Mars
- Invalides + Napoleon's tomb
- Rue Cler for dinner

### Day 4: Left Bank Culture
- Musée d'Orsay (impressionists)
- Latin Quarter walk
- Luxembourg Gardens
- Panthéon
- Saint-Germain dinner

### Day 5: Montmartre + North
- Sacré-Cœur early
- Montmartre village
- Pigalle/South 18th
- Canal Saint-Martin afternoon
- Dinner in 10th/11th

### Day 6: Day Trip
**Option A:** Versailles (full day, book ahead)
**Option B:** Giverny (Monet's gardens, spring-fall only)
**Option C:** Champagne region (wine tasting)

### Day 7: Personal Interests + Departure
- Sleep in
- Missed neighborhoods
- Final shopping
- Museum of choice
- Departure prep
