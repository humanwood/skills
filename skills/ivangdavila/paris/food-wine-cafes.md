## Apéro Culture

### What It Is

**Apéro** (apéritif) = pre-dinner drinks + snacks. 18:00-20:00 typically.

| Classic Drink | Notes |
|---------------|-------|
| **Kir** | White wine + cassis |
| **Kir Royal** | Champagne + cassis |
| **Pastis** | Anise liquor + water |
| **Vermouth** | Making comeback |
| **Champagne** | For celebrations |

## Practical Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Standing = cheaper** | At bar vs. seated |
| **Terrace = pricier** | +20-30% vs inside |
| **Happy hour** | 17:00-20:00, 50% off |
| **Carafe d'eau** | Free tap water |
| **Don't tip** | Service included |
| **Dress code** | None at most wine bars |
