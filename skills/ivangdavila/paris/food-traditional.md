# French Classics — Paris

## Essential Dishes

### Breakfast

| Item | What It Is | Where |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Croissant** | Buttery, flaky pastry | Any boulangerie with queue |
| **Pain au chocolat** | Chocolate croissant | Same |
| **Tartine** | Bread + butter + jam | Cafés |
| **Café crème** | Coffee with milk | Any café |

**Tip:** Look for "beurre" (butter) croissants. Avoid pre-made ones in cafés.

### Cheese Course

| Cheese Type | Example | Notes |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| **Soft** | Brie, Camembert | Room temperature |
| **Hard** | Comté, Gruyère | Aged varieties best |
| **Blue** | Roquefort | Strong |
| **Goat** | Chèvre | Fresh or aged |

**Tip:** Order "une assiette de fromages" at end of meal.

## Wine Basics

| Type | Common | Pairs With |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Rouge (red)** | Bordeaux, Burgundy, Côtes du Rhône | Beef, duck, cheese |
| **Blanc (white)** | Chablis, Sancerre, Alsace | Fish, chicken |
| **Rosé** | Provence | Summer, light food |
| **Champagne** | Various | Celebration, oysters |

**Ordering:** "Un verre de vin rouge/blanc" (a glass of red/white wine)
