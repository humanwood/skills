# Best Food Areas — Paris

## Quick Guide

| Area | Best For | Vibe |
|------|----------|------|
| **Marais (3rd-4th)** | Jewish deli, trendy | Diverse, busy |
| **Saint-Germain (6th)** | Classic cafés, upscale | Literary, elegant |
| **Latin Quarter (5th)** | Budget, student | Avoid tourist traps |
| **Rue Cler (7th)** | Market street, local | Charming, quality |
| **Oberkampf (11th)** | Bars, modern | Hip, nightlife |
| **Canal Saint-Martin (10th)** | Cafés, brunch | Trendy, young |
| **Belleville (20th)** | Asian, North African | Authentic, diverse |

---

## Marais (3rd-4th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Jewish deli** | L'As du Fallafel, Miznon |
| **Brunch** | Season, Café Charlot |
| **Wine bars** | Le Mary Celeste, Candelaria (hidden) |
| **Pastry** | Jacques Genin, Café Pinson |
| **Trendy** | Derrière, Breizh Café |

**Best street:** Rue des Rosiers (Jewish), Rue Vieille du Temple (bars)

---

## Saint-Germain (6th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Historic cafés** | Café de Flore, Les Deux Magots |
| **Chocolate** | Patrick Roger, Pierre Hermé |
| **Fine dining** | Le Comptoir, Fish La Boissonnerie |
| **Markets** | Marché Raspail (organic Sunday) |

**Note:** Expensive area. Worth visiting for atmosphere, not best value.

---

## Latin Quarter (5th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Budget** | Au Petit Grec (gyros) |
| **Wine bar** | Les Papilles |
| **Bakery** | Boulangerie Julien |

**Warning:** Avoid restaurants with pictures outside and "tourist menus." Walk to side streets.

---

## Rue Cler (7th)

Local market street near Eiffel Tower. Quality food shops, cafés.

| Type | Shop |
|------|------|
| **Cheese** | Marie Anne Cantin |
| **Bakery** | Secco |
| **Wine** | Cave Cler |
| **Lunch** | Le Petit Cler |

**Tip:** Best alternative to overpriced Eiffel Tower restaurants.

---

## Canal Saint-Martin (10th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Coffee** | Ten Belles, Café Craft |
| **Brunch** | Holybelly, Hardware Société |
| **Wine** | Le Verre Volé |
| **Dinner** | Pink Mamma (Italian), Chez Prune |

**Vibe:** Hipster, young professionals, cafés along canal.

---

## Oberkampf/Bastille (11th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Cocktails** | Septime La Cave, Combat |
| **Wine bars** | Le Dauphin, Yard |
| **Tapas** | Le Servan |
| **Natural wine** | Les Caves Populaires |

**Best for:** Nightlife, modern French, natural wine scene.

---

## Belleville (19th-20th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Chinese** | Chez Hé, Lao Siam |
| **North African** | Local couscous places |
| **Views** | Parc de Belleville |
| **Diverse** | Walk Rue de Belleville |

**Vibe:** Working class, multicultural, authentic.

---

## Chinatown (13th)

| Type | Recommendations |
|------|-----------------|
| **Pho** | Pho 14, Pho Banh Cuon 14 |
| **Dim sum** | Tang Frères (supermarket), restaurants on Avenue de Choisy |
| **Korean** | Along Rue Tolbiac |

**Best for:** Cheap, authentic Asian food.
