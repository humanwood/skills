## Freshwater

### Bass (Largemouth)
- **Habitat:** lakes, ponds, slow rivers with cover
- **Best baits:** plastic worms, crankbaits, spinnerbaits, topwater frogs
- **Peak season:** spring spawn (water 60-75F)
- **Behavior:** ambush predator, hides near structure

### Bass (Smallmouth)
- **Habitat:** clear lakes, rocky rivers with current
- **Best baits:** tube jigs, crawfish imitations, jerkbaits
- **Peak season:** late spring through fall
- **Behavior:** aggressive fighters, deeper than largemouth

### Trout (Rainbow)
- **Habitat:** cold streams, lakes with good oxygen
- **Best baits:** flies, inline spinners, PowerBait, worms
- **Peak season:** spring and fall
- **Behavior:** sight feeders, spook easily

### Trout (Brown)
- **Habitat:** streams with undercut banks, deeper pools
- **Best baits:** streamers, minnow imitations, night crawlers
- **Peak season:** fall spawning run
- **Behavior:** nocturnal, wary

### Catfish (Channel)
- **Habitat:** rivers, lakes, ponds with muddy bottoms
- **Best baits:** cut bait, chicken liver, stink baits
- **Peak season:** summer nights
- **Behavior:** bottom feeders, hunt by smell

### Walleye
- **Habitat:** large lakes, rivers with rocky structure
- **Best baits:** jigs with minnows, crankbaits, live bait rigs
- **Peak season:** spring and fall
- **Behavior:** low-light feeders

### Crappie
- **Habitat:** brush piles, submerged timber, docks
- **Best baits:** small jigs, minnows, tube baits
- **Peak season:** spring spawn
- **Behavior:** school fish

### Bluegill
- **Habitat:** weedy areas, docks, shallow water
- **Best baits:** worms, crickets, small jigs
- **Peak season:** late spring bedding
- **Behavior:** aggressive when spawning

## Saltwater

### Redfish
- **Habitat:** flats, marshes, jetties
- **Best baits:** shrimp, cut mullet, gold spoons
- **Peak season:** fall
- **Behavior:** tails when feeding shallow

### Snook
- **Habitat:** mangroves, bridges, inlets
- **Best baits:** live pilchards, swimbaits, topwater
- **Peak season:** summer
- **Behavior:** ambush feeders

### Striped Bass
- **Habitat:** coastal waters, rivers during spawn
- **Best baits:** bunker, eels, bucktails
- **Peak season:** spring and fall migrations
- **Behavior:** follow baitfish schools

### Flounder
- **Habitat:** sandy bottoms, structure edges
- **Best baits:** live minnows, bucktails, gulp baits
- **Peak season:** fall migration
- **Behavior:** lie in wait, ambush from below
