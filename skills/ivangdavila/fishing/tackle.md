## Line Guide

| Type | Stretch | Visibility | Best Use |
|------|---------|------------|----------|
| Mono | High | Moderate | Topwater, general |

### Line Weight by Species
- Panfish: 4-6 lb
- Bass: 10-17 lb
- Pike/Musky: 30-65 lb braid
- Catfish: 20-50 lb
- Inshore salt: 15-30 lb

## Presentations

### Texas Rig
Weedless soft plastic. Hook point in head, rotate, bury in body.

### Carolina Rig
Bottom contact with floating bait. Egg sinker, bead, swivel, 2-4ft leader.

### Drop Shot
Suspended finesse. Hook tied mid-line, weight at bottom.

### Wacky Rig
Natural fall. Hook through center of stick bait, no weight.

## Conditions Guide

| Condition | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| Clear water | Light line, natural colors |
| Muddy water | Bright colors, rattle baits |
| Cold front | Slow down, downsize |
| Warm front | Speed up, cover water |
| Wind | Fish windblown banks |
| Low light | Topwater, dark colors |
