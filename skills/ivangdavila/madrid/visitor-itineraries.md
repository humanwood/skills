# Itineraries — Madrid

## 1-Day Itinerary: Essential Madrid

For visitors with very limited time. Hits the highlights.

### Morning (9am-1pm)
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:00 | Breakfast | Coffee + tostada at any local bar |
| 10:00 | **Museo del Prado** | Focus on Velázquez (Las Meninas), Goya, Bosch |
| 12:30 | Walk through **Retiro Park** | Crystal Palace, rowboats if time |

### Afternoon (1pm-5pm)
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 13:30 | Lunch: **Menú del día** | Any restaurant in Huertas area, €12-15 |
| 15:00 | Walk **Paseo del Prado → Cibeles → Gran Vía** | Beautiful boulevard |
| 16:00 | Explore **Malasaña** | Coffee, vintage shops |

### Evening (5pm-11pm)
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 17:30 | **Templo de Debod** | Arrive for sunset |
| 19:00 | Walk to **Plaza Mayor → Sol** | Photo ops |
| 20:00 | Pre-dinner drinks | Vermouth at La Ardosa (Malasaña) |
| 21:30 | Tapas dinner | La Latina (Cava Baja) |

## Sunday Special Itinerary

Sunday in Madrid has a specific rhythm.

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:30 | **El Rastro** flea market | Arrive early, less crowded |
| 12:00 | **Vermut** at bar | Traditional Sunday aperitif |
| 14:00 | **Tapas lunch** in La Latina | Cava Baja area |
| 17:00 | **Retiro Park** walk | Sunday families, street musicians |
| 19:00 | **Free museum hours** | Prado or Reina Sofía |
| 21:30 | Light dinner | Anywhere |
