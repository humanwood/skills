# Local Services Guide — Madrid

## Healthcare

### Public Healthcare (Sanidad Pública)

**Access:** Through Social Security enrollment (work, convenio especial, or EU card)

| Service | How to Access |
|---------|---------------|
| **GP (médico de cabecera)** | Centro de Salud assignment, book via Cita Previa |
| **Specialist** | Referral from GP |
| **Emergency (Urgencias)** | Any hospital, 24/7, no appointment |
| **Mental health** | Referral from GP |

**Booking appointments:**
- App: Cita Sanitaria Madrid
- Phone: 900 102 112
- Online: comunidad.madrid/cita-previa

### Hospitals

| Hospital | Type | Location | Notes |
|----------|------|----------|-------|
| **Hospital La Paz** | Public, major | Chamartín | Largest, excellent |
| **Hospital 12 de Octubre** | Public, major | Usera | Major teaching hospital |
| **Hospital Gregorio Marañón** | Public, major | Retiro | Central |
| **Clínica Universitaria de Navarra** | Private | Chamartín | Premium |
| **Hospital Ruber** | Private | Chamartín | Premium |
| **HM Hospitales** | Private chain | Multiple | Good private option |

### Pharmacies (Farmacias)

- **Green cross** = pharmacy
- **24-hour pharmacies:** At least one per district, rotating
- **Find nearest:** farmacia.es or pharmacy apps
- **What pharmacists can do:** Recommend meds for minor issues, no prescription needed for many things

### Dental

Public dental limited to extractions and emergencies. Most people use private.

| Clinic Type | Cost (Checkup) | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-------|
| **Public** | Free | Very limited |
| **Private chain** | €30-50 | Vitaldent, Asisa Dental |
| **Private dentist** | €40-80 | Quality varies |

### Mental Health

| Resource | Access |
|----------|--------|
| **Public psychologist** | GP referral (long waits) |
| **Private therapy** | €50-100/session |
| **Crisis line** | 024 (suicide prevention) |
| **Teléfono de la Esperanza** | 717 003 717 |

## Pets

### Veterinarians

- Many neighborhood vets
- Emergency 24h: Hospital Veterinario de Madrid, others
- Cost: €30-50 routine visit

### Pet Registration

**Required:**
- Microchip
- Register at Ayuntamiento
- Annual fee: ~€20-40

### Pet-Friendly Places

- **Parks:** Dog areas in most parks
- **Restaurants:** Many terrazas allow dogs
- **Transport:** Small pets in carriers allowed on metro/bus
