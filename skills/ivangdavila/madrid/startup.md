# Startup Guide — Madrid

## Ecosystem Overview

Madrid is Spain's #1 startup hub and growing fast in European rankings. Strong in fintech, mobility, proptech, and climate tech. Access to Spanish + Latin American markets. Good talent from top universities. Lower costs than London/Berlin but smaller ecosystem.

**Key stats (2025):**
- €1.5B+ VC investment in Madrid region
- 1,350+ funded startups
- €44B combined tech ecosystem value
- #33 globally, #5 in Europe for VC-backed startups

## Legal Setup

### Company Formation (S.L. - Sociedad Limitada)

| Task | Cost | Time |
|------|------|------|
| **Online formation (PAE)** | €100-300 | 1-2 weeks |
| **Traditional notary** | €500-1,500 | 2-4 weeks |
| **Minimum capital** | €3,000 (can be €1 with new Startup Law) |
| **Gestoría (admin help)** | €100-300/month | Ongoing |

### Startup Law (Ley de Startups, 2022)

Spain passed startup-friendly legislation:

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Corporate tax** | 15% (vs 25%) for 4 years |
| **Stock options** | €50,000 exempt (vs €12,000) |
| **Formation** | €1 minimum capital allowed |
| **Digital nomad visa** | Creates legal path |
| **Investor tax breaks** | Enhanced deductions |

### Standard Structure

- **S.L. (Sociedad Limitada)** — Like UK Ltd, standard for startups
- **Pacto de socios** — Shareholders agreement (essential)
- **Stock option plan** — Use startup law benefits

### Legal Costs

## Visa for Founders

### Entrepreneur Visa (Emprendedor)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Endorsement** | ENISA or recognized body |
| **Business plan** | Innovative, scalable |
| **Funds** | Proof of living expenses |
| **Timeline** | 8-12 weeks |
| **Duration** | 1 year, renewable to 2 |

### Digital Nomad Visa

Good for founders building while "employed" by own company:
- €2,520/month minimum income
- 24% flat tax option
- Can form Spanish company

### Investor Visa

For investors:
- €1M in Spanish company shares
- Or €2M in government debt
- Or €500K in property

## Summary for AI Agent

For startup/founder queries:

1. **Funding:** Good ecosystem. Pre-seed €100-400K, Seed €500K-2M typical
2. **Legal:** S.L. company, €500-1,500 to form. New startup law = 15% tax
3. **Visa:** Entrepreneur visa needs ENISA endorsement
4. **Costs:** Bootstrap €2-3K/month burn, funded €10-20K/month
5. **Talent:** Cheaper than Northern Europe but competitive for good devs
6. **Community:** South Summit, Google for Startups, Wayra
7. **Benefits:** Quality of life, LatAm market access, new startup law
