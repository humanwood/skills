## Healthcare (Free!)

### How It Works

1. Register with Social Security (through university or work)
2. Get assigned a Centro de Salud
3. Access public healthcare free

### If You Don't Have Social Security

| Option | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **European Health Card (EHIC/TSE)** | EU students covered |
| **Private insurance** | Required for visa if non-EU |
| **Convenio especial** | Pay-in option for public |

### Common Needs

| Need | Where |
|------|-------|
| **General doctor** | Centro de Salud (book appointment) |
| **Urgent care** | Urgencias at any hospital |
| **Mental health** | University psychology services (free) |
| **Pharmacy** | Can recommend for minor issues |

## Student Visa (Non-EU)

### Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Acceptance letter** | From Spanish university |
| **Funds proof** | ~€600/month for duration |
| **Health insurance** | Full coverage in Spain |
| **Accommodation** | Proof of where you'll live |
| **Clean record** | Police certificate |
| **Cost** | €80-100 |

### Residence Card (TIE)

After arrival, within 30 days:
1. Apply for TIE (Tarjeta de Identidad de Extranjero)
2. Need: Padron (registration), photos, proof of enrollment
3. Allows multiple entries to Spain
