## Apartments & Rentals

### When Apartments Make Sense
- Stays 5+ nights
- Families or groups
- Want kitchen/space
- Prefer local living

### Platforms

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **Airbnb** | Variety, reviews |
| **Booking.com Apartments** | Reliability, support |
| **Spotahome** | Verified properties |
| **Flatio** | Mid-term stays |

### Typical Prices (Central)

| Type | Per Night | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Studio | €70-120 | 1-2 people |
| 1-Bedroom | €100-180 | 2-3 people |
| 2-Bedroom | €150-250 | 4-5 people |

### Apartment Red Flags
- No reviews
- Prices too good
- Payment outside platform
- "Keys at café" arrangements
- No interior photos

### Legal Note
Tourist apartments need license (VUT number). Licensed = safer.

## Booking Tips

### When to Book
| Timing | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **2-3 months ahead** | Major events (San Isidro, Pride) |
| **1 month ahead** | Peak season (Easter, fall) |
| **2 weeks ahead** | Normal times |
| **Last minute** | Off-season deals possible |

### Peak Seasons (Higher Prices)
- Easter week (Semana Santa)
- May (San Isidro, good weather)
- September-October (best weather)
- Christmas/New Year
- Pride (late June)

### Low Season (Better Deals)
- January-February (except holidays)
- July-August (but HOT)
- November

### Money-Saving Tips

| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| Book directly with hotel | Often 5-10% off, better rooms |
| Sunday-Thursday stays | Lower than Fri-Sat |
| Check "member" rates | Booking.com Genius, etc. |
| Consider outer areas | Arganzuela, Tetuán still central |
| Hostels with private rooms | Hotel quality, hostel price |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It's Bad |
|---------|--------------|
| Staying too far from center | Madrid is best walked |
| Sol expecting quiet | Tourist central, noisy |
| Salamanca expecting nightlife | It's quiet after dinner |
| Not checking noise | Ask for quiet room if near bars |
| Airport hotels | Not necessary, city is 30 min away |
