# Cost of Living Guide — Madrid

## Overview

Madrid is expensive by Spanish standards but affordable compared to London, Paris, or Munich. Housing is the biggest cost. Quality of life per euro is excellent — world-class culture, food, and weather for reasonable prices.

**Comparison:** 
- ~40% cheaper than London
- ~30% cheaper than Paris
- ~20% cheaper than Barcelona (housing)
- ~10% cheaper than Munich

| **Water** | €20-35 |
| **Internet** | €30-50 |
| **Mobile** | €15-30 |
| **Contents insurance** | €10-20 |

**Total utilities:** €130-250/month for 1-bed

### Community Fees (Comunidad)

If renting: usually included or landlord pays
If owning: €50-150/month for maintenance, cleaning, elevator

### No Council Tax!

Unlike UK, Spain has no council tax for renters. Owners pay IBI (property tax) ~0.4-1.1% of cadastral value annually.

## Money-Saving Tips

### Housing
| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| Live outside M-30 | Save €200-400/month |
| Flat share | Save €500-800/month vs solo |
| Negotiate rent at renewal | Often 5-10% possible |

### Food
| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| Menú del día for lunch | €5-10 vs à la carte |
| Shop at Mercadona/Lidl | 20-30% vs El Corte Inglés |
| Avoid touristy restaurants | 30-50% markup |

### Transport
| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| Get Abono | Better than pay-per-trip |
| Under 26? Abono Joven | €10/month unlimited! |
| BiciMAD for short trips | €0.50 vs €1.50 metro |

### Entertainment
| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| Free museum hours | €10-15 per visit |
| Día del Espectador (cinema) | 30-40% off |
| Vermouth before lunch | Cheaper than dinner drinks |
