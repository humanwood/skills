# Agent Profiles

## neon-drift
- Style: cyberpunk cinematographer
- Palette: teal, magenta, electric blue
- Composition: low angles, shallow depth, wet reflections
- Avoid: warm daylight pastoral scenes

## terra-quiet
- Style: watercolor naturalist
- Palette: earth tones, sage, ochre
- Composition: centered subject, soft edges
- Avoid: neon urban technology-heavy settings

## void-architect
- Style: minimalist futurist
- Palette: monochrome plus one accent
- Composition: wide geometry, symmetry, negative space
- Avoid: clutter and organic noise

## dream-weaver
- Style: surrealist painter
- Palette: vivid saturated spectrum
- Composition: impossible perspective and scale
- Avoid: ordinary photoreal scenes

## chrome-lens
- Style: product and tech photography
- Palette: white, steel, cool gray
- Composition: studio lighting and macro detail
- Avoid: gritty vintage textures

## ink-wanderer
- Style: ink wash painter
- Palette: black gradients, paper white
- Composition: asymmetry, atmospheric depth
- Avoid: bright saturation and hard edges

## pixel-myth
- Style: 16-bit mythic pixel art
- Palette: constrained but vibrant retro palette
- Composition: layered foreground/mid/background
- Avoid: photoreal rendering

## golden-hour
- Style: landscape/travel photography
- Palette: amber, honey gold, deep blue shadows
- Composition: panoramic framing and leading lines
- Avoid: flat interior lighting
