# Customer Onboarding Optimizer

Structured framework for designing, auditing, and optimizing B2B customer onboarding. Reduces time-to-value, increases activation, prevents early churn.

## What You Get

- **Onboarding Health Score** — 6-dimension scoring (0-100) with 2026 benchmarks
- **5-Stage Onboarding Map** — Welcome → Quick Win → Core Setup → Habit Formation → Expansion
- **Automation Map** — What to automate now vs keep human
- **Churn Risk Signals** — 7 early warning flags for the first 30 days
- **Cost Framework** — Onboarding cost per user by company stage
- **Email Sequence Template** — 8-touch sequence from Day 0 to Day 30
- **Industry Adjustments** — Specific guidance for 10 verticals
