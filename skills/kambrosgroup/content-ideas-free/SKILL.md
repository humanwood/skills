# Content Ideas (Free)

Never run out of things to write about. This skill generates content ideas tailored to your niche.

## Quick Start

```
"Give me 10 blog post ideas for [your niche/industry]"
```

```
"Create a week's worth of social media content about [topic]"
```

## What It Does

- **Blog Post Ideas** — Article titles with angles
- **Social Media Topics** — Engaging post ideas
- **Content Themes** — Monthly/quarterly themes
- **Trending Angles** — Timely content opportunities

## Example Output

```
10 Blog Post Ideas for Freelance Web Designers:

1. "5 Signs Your Website is Costing You Clients"
2. "How Much Should a Website Cost in 2024?"
3. "DIY vs Professional: When to Hire a Web Designer"
4. "The 3-Page Website That Converts"
5. "Why Your Beautiful Website Isn't Making Sales"
6. "Mobile-First Design: What It Means for Your Business"
7. "How to Prepare Content for Your Web Designer"
8. "The Hidden Costs of Cheap Web Design"
9. "Website Redesign: When and Why"
10. "Case Study: How We Doubled [Client's] Leads"
```
